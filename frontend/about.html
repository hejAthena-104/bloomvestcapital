About

We help people move and manage their money with modern, secure digital banking.

Bloomvest Capital is a digital banking platform built for people who want simple, secure control of their money. From one account you can send local and international transfers, swap between USD and BTC, spend with virtual and physical cards, and access loans and grants.

From your first deposit to every transfer, the experience is transparent, secure and fully under your control. We combine everyday banking with bank-grade security to make managing money simple for everyone.

  • Local and international money transfers
  • Instant USD to BTC currency swaps
  • Virtual and physical cards
  • Secure deposits and fast withdrawals
Our approach

A trusted way to send, swap, and spend your money

Our mission is to make modern banking accessible to people in 170+ countries. We handle the security and infrastructure, while you send transfers, swap currencies, and spend with your cards from a single, simple account.

  • Send local and international transfers with saved beneficiaries.
  • Swap instantly between USD and BTC at clear rates.
  • Spend online and in store with virtual and physical cards.
  • Track your money live and withdraw your funds quickly and securely whenever you need them.
