FAQs

Find your answers faster in our popular FAQs topics

  • What is Bloomvest Capital?

    Bloomvest Capital is a digital-asset investment platform. You fund your account with crypto, choose a professionally managed plan, and earn a fixed daily return that is credited straight to your portfolio.

  • How do I start investing?

    Open an account, complete a quick verification, deposit using USDT, Bitcoin, Ethereum or Litecoin, then select the investment plan that matches your budget and goals to begin earning daily ROI.

  • How are daily returns calculated?

    Each plan carries a fixed daily ROI, ranging from 5% on the Starter plan up to 20% on VIP. Your return is applied to your invested amount every day for the duration of your chosen plan term.

  • How do I withdraw my profits?

    Request a withdrawal from your dashboard at any time and your funds are sent securely to your chosen crypto wallet. Withdrawals are processed quickly so you always stay in control of your money.

  • Which cryptocurrencies can I deposit?

    You can fund your Bloomvest account using USDT (TRC20), Bitcoin, Ethereum or Litecoin. Simply choose your preferred coin at deposit and send to the wallet address shown on your dashboard.

  • Is my investment secure?

    Yes. We protect your account and funds with bank-grade security, encrypted wallets, strict access controls and continuous monitoring, backed by 99.9% uptime and 24/7 support.

  • What is the minimum to get started?

    You can begin with as little as $100 on the Starter plan. As your confidence grows, you can move up to higher tiers with larger investment ranges and stronger daily returns.

  • How does the referral program work?

    Share your referral link and earn commission rewards when the people you invite fund their accounts and invest, giving you an extra income stream alongside your own daily returns.
