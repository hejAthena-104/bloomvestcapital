Managed investment plans put your crypto to work and pay you a fixed daily return, automatically

What are managed investment plans?

A managed investment plan is the simplest way to grow your digital assets with Bloomvest Capital. You choose a plan that fits your budget, fund it with crypto, and our team handles the strategy and risk management. From that moment your capital starts earning a fixed daily ROI that is credited directly to your portfolio for the full term of the plan.

Built for every kind of investor

Whether you are investing for the first time or moving a larger portfolio, there is a plan tier designed for you. Smaller plans let you start safely and learn how the platform works, while higher tiers reward larger commitments with stronger daily returns. You stay in full control, with a real-time dashboard and the freedom to withdraw your profits when it suits you.

How a plan works

Getting started takes only a few minutes. Once your deposit is confirmed and your plan is active, your daily returns are calculated automatically and added to your balance every day until the term completes.

  • Open and verify your account
  • Deposit with USDT, BTC, ETH or LTC
  • Choose your investment plan
  • Earn a fixed daily ROI
  • 24/7 support and monitoring
  • Withdraw your profits anytime

The benefits of investing with Bloomvest Capital

Our plans are designed to make crypto investing predictable and stress-free. You earn consistent daily returns without having to time the market or actively trade, your funds are protected by bank-grade security, and every movement is transparent on your dashboard. With investors in 170+ countries and over $50M in assets under management, you are investing alongside a trusted, global community.

Choose the plan that fits your goals

Bloomvest Capital offers five plan tiers. Starter pays 5% daily for 7 days on investments of $100 to $999. Standard pays 7.5% daily for 14 days from $1,000 to $4,999. Professional pays 10% daily for 21 days from $5,000 to $14,999. Retirement pays 15% daily for 30 days from $15,000 to $49,999. VIP pays 20% daily for 30 days on investments of $50,000 and above. Whichever tier you select, returns are credited every day and your portfolio updates in real time.

  • When do I receive my daily returns?

    Your ROI is calculated and credited to your portfolio every day for the entire term of your plan, so you can watch your balance grow without any manual action on your part.

  • Can I run more than one plan at a time?

    Yes. You can hold multiple active plans across different tiers, and your dashboard shows the combined daily returns and total value of your entire portfolio in real time.

  • How do I withdraw my profits?

    Request a withdrawal at any time from your dashboard and your funds are sent securely to your chosen crypto wallet. Withdrawals are processed quickly so your profits are always within reach.
