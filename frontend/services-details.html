Send money locally and across borders in minutes, securely and at clear, upfront rates

What are money transfers?

Money transfers are the simplest way to move funds with Bloomvest Capital. From one account you can pay friends, family, and businesses at home or in another country. Add a recipient, enter the amount, and confirm. Your transfer is sent securely with the rate shown up front, and most payments arrive in minutes.

Built for everyday payments

Whether you are splitting a bill or supporting family overseas, sending money should be effortless. Save the people and businesses you pay most often as beneficiaries so repeat transfers take just a tap, with no need to re-enter details. You stay in full control, with a real-time dashboard and a clear record of every payment.

How a transfer works

Getting started takes only a few minutes. Once your account is funded, choose a saved beneficiary or add a new one, enter the amount, and confirm. Your transfer is sent securely with the rate shown before you send.

  • Open and verify your account
  • Add funds to your balance
  • Choose or save a beneficiary
  • Send locally or internationally
  • 24/7 support and monitoring
  • Track every payment in real time

The benefits of sending with Bloomvest Capital

Our transfers are designed to make moving money fast and stress-free. You send to anyone at home or abroad in minutes, with clear rates and no hidden fees, while your funds are protected by bank-grade security and every payment is transparent on your dashboard. With customers in 170+ countries and over $50M moved, you are banking alongside a trusted, global community.

More ways to move your money

Transfers are just the start. Swap instantly between USD and Crypto at clear rates, spend online and in store with virtual and physical cards, and access loans and grants directly from your account. Deposits and withdrawals are fast and secure, and saved beneficiaries make repeat payments effortless. Whatever you need, every action is tracked on your dashboard in real time.

  • How long does a transfer take?

    Most transfers arrive within minutes. Timing can vary for some international destinations, but you always see an estimate and the rate before you confirm, with no hidden steps.

  • Can I save beneficiaries for repeat transfers?

    Yes. Save the people and businesses you pay most often, and your dashboard keeps them ready so future transfers take just a tap, with no need to re-enter their details.

  • How do I withdraw my funds?

    Request a withdrawal at any time from your dashboard and your funds are sent securely to your chosen destination. Withdrawals are processed quickly so your money is always within reach.
