Virtual cards

Spin up a virtual card in seconds and start spending online right away.

Create a Bloomvest Capital virtual card directly from your dashboard and use it instantly for online shopping, subscriptions, and global payments. No waiting for delivery, no paperwork, just a secure card number ready when you are.

  • Issued instantly, no shipping wait.
  • Works with merchants worldwide.
  • Freeze, unfreeze, or delete a card anytime.
  • Set spending limits to stay in control of every transaction.
Physical cards

Order a physical card and tap, swipe, or withdraw anywhere your card is accepted.

For everyday spending in stores and at ATMs, request a Bloomvest Capital physical card from your dashboard. It is linked to the same balance as your virtual cards, so funds move seamlessly between online and in-person purchases.

  • Accepted at millions of merchants and ATMs.
  • Contactless payments for fast, secure checkout.
  • Real-time notifications on every transaction.
  • Lock your card instantly if it is ever lost or stolen.
